Privacy Policy

TFS Form Pilot is a browser extension designed to assist users with job application forms.

Data Collection

The extension may store user-provided information such as names, email addresses, and job application details locally in the user's browser using Chrome storage. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Website Content

The extension interacts with website content solely to identify and fill job application form fields on pages visited by the user.

Data Sharing

No user data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

Data Security

All data remains on the user's device and can be removed at any time by uninstalling the extension.
